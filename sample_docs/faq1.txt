Q: How do I reset my password?
A: To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. 
You will receive an email with a reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to set a new password.

Q: How long does the password reset link remain valid?
A: The reset link remains valid for 30 minutes from the time it is issued.

Q: I did not receive my reset email. What should I do?
A: Check your spam folder and ensure you are using the correct registered email address. 
If the issue persists, contact customer support.
